The theory of plate tectonics is a theory that was tried to be proved by a man named Alfred Wegener. In the 1900, people believed that he was crazy, like all other people who tried to prove an excellent theory. Plate tectonics is the theory that the outer rigid layer of the earth (the lithosphere) is divided into a couple of dozen "plates" that move around across the earth's surface relative to each other, like slabs of ice on a lake.
